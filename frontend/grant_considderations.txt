Making Your Application Stand Out

Clear Value Proposition: Emphasize the unique value proposition of your project. How will it benefit Osmosis users, LPs, and the broader ecosystem? For example:
Improved Governance: Explain how your reputation system can lead to more informed and effective governance decisions in Osmosis DAOs.
Enhanced Security: Highlight how decentralized identities can improve security and reduce fraud in DeFi interactions.
New Opportunities: Describe how your system can unlock new opportunities for users, such as accessing exclusive pools or earning higher rewards based on their reputation.
Technical Strengths: Showcase the technical strengths of your approach, including the use of CosmJS, IBC, and potentially zero-knowledge proofs. Demonstrate a clear understanding of the Cosmos SDK and Osmosis technology.
Community Focus: If you've started building a community around your project, mention that in your application. Early community support can be a positive signal for the grant committee.
Strong Team: If you have a team with relevant experience, highlight their skills and expertise in blockchain development, DeFi, and/or identity management.
Feasibility: Present a realistic roadmap with achievable milestones. Focus on delivering a functional MVP (Minimum Viable Product) within a reasonable timeframe.
Budget Transparency: Provide a clear and detailed budget breakdown, explaining how the grant funds will be used to achieve your project goals.
